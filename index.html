About Us

Bamiolat Integrated Service is a Nigerian indigenous company delivering professional solutions in Information Technology and Agriculture. We specialize in ICT training, supply of IT equipment, computer maintenance and repairs, as well as crop and animal farming.
Our goal is to empower individuals, businesses, and organizations with practical digital skills and reliable technology services while supporting sustainable agricultural development and food production. Through innovation, quality service delivery, and industry expertise, Bamiolat Integrated Service is committed to contributing to digital growth and agricultural sustainability in Nigeria.
